About Me

Building for the Web
Since 2015

I'm Nathaniel Kenny Olie, a freelance web developer based in Warri, Delta State. I've been building websites since 2015 — hand-coding every line of HTML and CSS to create fast, reliable, and beautiful web experiences.

I specialise in hotel, church, and business websites for Nigerian clients. My philosophy is simple: no page builders, no WordPress vulnerabilities, no bloated code. Just clean, professional sites that load instantly and rank well on Google.

When you hire me, you get a real developer who answers WhatsApp messages and delivers on time. Based in Warri — reachable anytime.
