About Me

Hand-Coding the Web,
One Clean Line at a Time

10+
Years Experience

I'm Nathaniel Kenny Olie, a freelance web developer with over a decade of experience building fast, conversion-focused websites from scratch — no page builders, no WordPress, no bloat. Just clean, hand-crafted HTML, CSS, and JavaScript that performs.

I work with clients worldwide, delivering websites that don't just look good — they load fast, rank on Google, and turn visitors into paying customers.

What I Build

Every project I take on is built with a focus on three things: speed, visibility, and results. Whether you need a business website, a church platform, a hotel booking page, or a professional portfolio, I deliver a site that works hard for you around the clock.

My Technical Stack

I'm proficient in HTML5, CSS3, and responsive design — meaning your site looks sharp on every screen size. Beyond the build, I handle SEO, PageSpeed optimisation, and Google Search Console indexing so your site doesn't just exist online — it gets found.

I also set up Google Business profiles, implement web accessibility standards, manage version control with Git, and build automated email workflows using n8n — giving your business the kind of backend automation most freelancers don't even offer.

For clients who need content, I write long-form SEO blog posts that rank — detailed, researched, and optimised to drive organic traffic month after month.
